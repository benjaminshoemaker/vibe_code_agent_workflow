# Idea Snapshot

## Problem
Founders lack a guided workflow that turns an idea into agent-ready docs.

## Audience
Solo builders and very small teams validating new product directions.

## Platform
Single-origin Next.js app with Fastify APIs.

## Core Flow
Collect an idea, iterate through gated stages, and export docs/designs as a flat ZIP.

## MVP Features
- Intake doc editor
- Stage approvals with validators
- Designs ZIP upload
- Export manifest and download
